About Every Woman Every Baby

Our mission to empower women through health education

Our Mission

EWEB's mission is to empower women and families by providing accessible, evidence-based health education focused on maternal and child health. Founded by registered nurses, EWEB is dedicated to improving women's health outcomes through knowledge-sharing, advocacy, and multilingual educational outreach—locally and globally.

Our Vision

Our vision is a future where every woman and child, regardless of their background, has access to the knowledge and resources they need to thrive. We envision a world where health education helps prevent illness, supports well-being, and enables individuals to make informed decisions about their care.

Our Story

Every Woman Every Baby Inc. (EWEB) is a non-profit organization founded by three dedicated registered nurses with a shared mission: to advance women's health through education, empowerment, and accessibility.

At EWEB, we believe that informed women are the foundation of healthy families and thriving communities. Our work centers on delivering accurate, culturally respectful health education to women across the globe—with a special focus on underserved populations.

By creating and sharing educational resources in local languages, we aim to break down barriers to health literacy and ensure every woman has the knowledge she needs to make informed decisions for herself and her family.
